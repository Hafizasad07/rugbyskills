About us
Welcome to Rugby Skillz

Rugby Skillz was founded by Wozza, a passionate coach who saw the need for additional time and focus on core rugby skills for kids. While the club structure was excellent, limitations in time, space, and resources meant some children needed or wanted more opportunities to develop. Rugby Skillz was created to provide those opportunities in a structured, enjoyable environment, free from the complications of traditional club rugby (and unpredictable weather!). The program continues to evolve, now incorporating nutrition, speed training, agility, and teamwork, with overwhelming support from local clubs and schools.
